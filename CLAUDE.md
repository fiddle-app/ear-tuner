# ear-tuner

See the parent CLAUDE.md (fiddle/CLAUDE.md) for full project context, data architecture, and coding conventions.

## Purpose

Test and document the users, oral acuity (or at least their ability to distinguish fine differences in pitch), as well as improve their aural acuity for the ultimate goal of improving their intonation when playing a fretless stringed instrument, singing, or playing another instrument that requires active intonation skills. The app should be easy to use without actually looking at the screen so that you can use it with your eyes closed or while driving

## Status

Version 1 complete, but developed outside of the unified "Fiddle apps" context. Ready to be used for helping to develop the standard look and feel for the family of apps.
